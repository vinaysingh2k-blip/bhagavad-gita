## Notes for Campaign Setup

- Use **Manual CPC or Maximize Conversions** bidding once you have 15+ conversions of history.
- Set **location targeting** to India (or specific states/cities you serve) for lower CPC and higher relevance.
- Enable **Enhanced Conversions** and tag the `success.html` page with a conversion event once Razorpay is live.
- Pair with a **Google Merchant Center / Shopping-style feed** later if you expand to multiple books.
- Track scroll depth + `#buy` section clicks in GA4 for Quality Score insight ahead of full conversion tracking.
